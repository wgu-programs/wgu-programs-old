## When to use

A learner needs to make a complex decision, taking into consideration a variety of factors.

## Pattern Requirements

A situation that requires a decision to be made

## Pattern Structure

### Explore a case

### Identify the decision

### Explore options

### Make a decision

### Receive feedback
